# Podcast

The podcast is the spoken part of the work on okbayat.com. Episodes are used to examine a question, source, experience, or concept in conversation; they do not replace written documentation or evidence review.

## [Inja-Anja](/voice/podcast)

«اینجا-آنجا» is a Persian-language podcast about worldview, frames of reference, perception, language, integrity, identity, and the possibility of change.

Where possible, an episode should connect to a canonical essay, research note, reading note, translation, source page, or program record so the question and its sources can continue to be revised in writing.
