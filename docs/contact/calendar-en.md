# Schedule a Meeting

Use the calendar below to find a potential meeting time. A visible opening is not a confirmed appointment.

To request a meeting:

1. Choose a suitable open time.
2. Email **[me@OkBayat.com](mailto:me@OkBayat.com)** or send a calendar invitation to that address.
3. Include the topic, purpose, and expected outcome of the conversation.

I will review the request against my priorities and availability. If I can accept it, I will reply with confirmation and any necessary details.
