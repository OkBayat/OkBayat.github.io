# Contact

For professional, research, project, or speaking inquiries, email **[me@OkBayat.com](mailto:me@OkBayat.com)**.

A useful message should include:

- who you are;
- the subject or project you are writing about;
- why you think a conversation or collaboration would be useful;
- the decision, question, or next step you have in mind.

## Scheduling

The [calendar page](/about/calendar) remains available as a scheduling utility, but it is not part of the site's main intellectual navigation. A visible opening does not confirm a meeting until the invitation has been reviewed and accepted.

## Privacy

Do not send confidential health, family, coaching, financial, or organizational information in an initial message. Share only what is necessary to establish whether the conversation is appropriate.
