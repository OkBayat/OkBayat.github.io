## Selection Standard

Inclusion here means that a work is relevant, published, and clear about its authorship and evidence status. It does not mean that the work confirms a preferred answer or has been peer reviewed. Inconclusive notes, revised interpretations, negative observations, and contradictory evidence remain useful when they are documented clearly.
