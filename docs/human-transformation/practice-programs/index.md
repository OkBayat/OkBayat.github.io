# Practice & Programs

This section documents facilitated programs as settings for practice and inquiry. A program record is not an enrollment page and is not evidence that every participant will experience the same result.

## Program Records

### [Mastery for Life](/human-transformation/practice-programs/mastery-for-life)

A record of the recurring program known in Persian as «چیرگی بر زندگی»: its purpose, questions, format, participant reports, evidence limits, and future documentation needs.

## Related Practice Archive

The existing [Leadership Programs archive](/leadership/courses) preserves course pages and historical material. Individual cohort pages may refer to past dates and should not be interpreted as current registration pages unless they explicitly carry a current date and status.

## Program Record Standard

A public program record should normally include:

1. source lineage and the author's contribution;
2. the questions or commitments organizing the program;
3. format, history, and facilitator role;
4. direct observations and participant self-reports, kept distinct;
5. what has and has not been measured;
6. limitations, privacy, and role conflicts;
7. revisions and future evaluation plans;
8. a separate operational link only when registration is currently open.
